Downloading and using the browser extension

This last step requires you to download the Footietracker extension.

Our browser extension updates your spreadsheet with your Football Index portfolio data and can be downloaded from your browser's addon store:

  • Chrome Web Store
  • Microsoft Edge Add-ons
  • Firefox Browser Add-ons

Once you have installed the extension, visit the Football Index website and login. After you have logged in, open the Footietracker extension. Chrome and Edge users can open the extension by clicking on the Footietracker icon at the top right of the browser window while Firefox users will see this icon to the right of the search/URL bar.

The extension will show two buttons when you visit footballindex.co.uk: one "simplified" and one "full" update button. The full version will update the spreadsheet with the expiry dates of your shares which can take a few minutes. For a quick update, click on the simplified version which should update the spreadsheet instantly.

Your spreadsheet can be found in your Google Sheets documents here.

For more information about our browser extension, please visit our browser extension instructions page
