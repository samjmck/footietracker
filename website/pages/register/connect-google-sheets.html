Authorise access to Google Sheets

Thank you for subscribing.

This step requires you to authorise Footietracker access to your Google Sheets. Once this has been done, a template spreadsheet named Footietracker will be created in your Sheets documents.

Your portfolio data will be saved in that document. It is also the only document that will be accessed by Footietracker in your Google Sheets documents.
